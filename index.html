Are World Cups Getting More Aggressive?

Cards per match rose steadily from their introduction in 1970, peaking at 2006 — infamously home to the "Battle of Nuremberg." Since then, aggression has declined as FIFA tightened disciplinary standards.

Continental Advantage

Does geography decide who lifts the trophy? When the World Cup is hosted in the Americas or Europe, the winner almost always comes from that same continent.
